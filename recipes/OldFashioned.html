Classic Old Fashioned

This old fashioned is the best cocktail to wash down a great meal. Made with Makers Mark Whiskey this beverage
to sit down, relax, and wind down to.


Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Maker's Mark Bourbon
  • Splash Water
  • 2 Dashes Bitters
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 Orange slice
  • Marashino Cherries

  1. Classic Old Fashinoned: Dissolve or muddle sugar with a splash of water in an Old Fashioned
    glass. Fill glass .75 full of ice. Add Makers's Mark Bourbon and bitters and
    stir. Garnish with orange slice and maraschino cherry.
  2. Muddled Old Fashioned: Muddle sugar, bitters, orange, cherry and water in an Old
    Fashioned glass. Remove the orange rind and fill glass .75 full of ice. Add Maker's Mark Bourbon and stir.
