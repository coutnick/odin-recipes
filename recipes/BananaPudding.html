No Bake Banana Pudding

This banana pudding is sure to make your loved ones happy. A perfect dessert for everyone!
With creamy vanilla pudding and crunchy Vanilla wafers this southren delicacy will be gone before you know it.


For this recipe we will need:

  • 1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 package vanilla wafers
  • 14 bananas, sliced

Directions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat pudding mix and milk 2 minutes. Blend in
    condensed milk until smooth. Stir in vanilla and fold in whipped topping.
    Layer wafers, bananas and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl. Chill until serving.s
