Apricot Meatloaf

This meatloaf is AMAZING!Every time it hits the table it vanishes before everyone's eyes.

I absolutley love making this flavorful dish that is sure to excite your tastebuds bite after bite.

Don't forget to let the meatloaf to sit. It is the most important step in the whole process.

For this recipe we will need:

Loaf:

  • 2lbs of lean ground beef
  • 1 cup Ritz Crackers
  • 1 small yellow onion, minced
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 tablespoons apricot preserves
  • 1 tablespoon koser salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons freshly ground pepper

Glaze:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1.5 cup apricot preserves
  • .25 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg, garlic, apricot preserves, salt, and
    pepper for the loaf in a large mixing bowl: mix well until thoroughly combined.
    Transfer to 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, mix ketchup, apricot preserves, brown sugar, hot sauce, Worcestershire
    sauce, and apple cider vinegar for the glaze together in a medium bowl.
  5. Remove meatloaf from the oven and pour glaze over top. Continue cooking until no
    longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes longer. An instant-read thermometer
    inserted into the center should read a least 160 degrees F. Let rest 5 muinutes
    before serving.
